Our Visionary

Meet Michael Graham

Michael Graham

CEO & Founder, CDM Enterprises

Strategic Leader | Advocate

Michael Graham is a self-made, multi-sector entrepreneur and the visionary CEO and Founder of CDM Enterprises, a strategic collective that drives excellence across finance, investment, real estate, and technology. Built from the foundation of C. D. Stroud & M. Graham Investments, Michael has established an expansive portfolio dedicated to helping small business owners and individuals secure capital, achieve operational mastery, and realize sustainable growth. He has empowered countless entrepreneurs to build financial security and unlock their full potential.

Originally from Philadelphia, PA, Michael established his diversified portfolio in Oklahoma City, OK. His leadership is defined by a rigorous business acumen coupled with a genuine commitment to empowering others. He oversees strategic mandates for all CDM Enterprises subsidiaries, spanning finance, consulting, capital, property management, and technology. Michael’s philosophy is centered on fostering growth—not just in business metrics, but in the lives of the people he serves, providing strategic advice on securing funding and mentoring individuals on developing a success-driven mindset.

Beyond his professional achievements, Michael maintains a deep commitment to his community. He is a proud and active member of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., where he is involved in initiatives promoting service, scholarship, and brotherhood. As a devoted family man, he ensures the values of integrity, loyalty, and love are the bedrock of both his personal life and his entire business ecosystem. Michael’s commitment to excellence and his unwavering desire to help others ascend make him a respected leader and a trusted advisor for those seeking to take their ventures to the next level.
